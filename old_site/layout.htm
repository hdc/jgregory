ADAM'S APPLE 1997 Concept-as-Word; Word-as Form In ADAM'S APPLE, a word which expresses movement, a season, or a condition of spiritual grace has been translated into a tangible form which manages to convey all this ambiguousness. Here is an eight-foot square wall installation which has been constructed of hundreds and hundreds of dried apple parts (seeds, stems, peels, cores) and tiny produce labels.
