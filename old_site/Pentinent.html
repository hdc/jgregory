VESTMENT SERIES: PENITENT

What forms does atonement take? Are we doomed to live lives marked by original sin or is perfection within reach of the fallen angel who keeps its wings hung, mitten-like, for quick retrieval when it's time to soar? Must the beggar's rags and self-flagellation be the only hope for redemption or is there another approach here we have yet to embrace?

Date: 2010
About 37"H x 69"W x 5"Deep
Twigs, Twine, handmade paper, acrylics
