The Garden In Winter

Acrylics on cast paper on canvas,
 cast-paper-encased dried flowers, thread

This is a piece which becomes a metaphor for life and its seasons of aging. It explores the beauty in decaying objects and looks for value in the obsolete.


(81 "H x 60"W x 1.5" Deep)
